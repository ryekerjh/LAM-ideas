100% Natural Sheepskin

100% Natural Sheepskin

Click to See Full Screen View

My sisters and I have always wanted the coziness of sheepskins in our homes, but have never been able to afford the space or funds for the large sheepskins. We decided to modify the large ones in order to bring the price and space requirements down to a more sensible, affordable level. Each of the sheepskins we offer has its own unique look because they were individually hand shaped and sized, but they are all approximately the same dimensions.

These decorative sheepskins have many possible uses; accent throws, chair cushions, headboard decorations, small area rugs, warmth on a chilly evening or simply to use for your own, unique purpose.

In many cultures sheepskins have been used in babies' beds to regulate their body temperatures, no matter the climate. They also provide parents with peace of mind if their babies are tummy sleepers because of the many air pockets created by the soft, downy wool. Due to the many regulations we are subject to under federal law we cannot officially recommend that you use these sheepskins traditionally as described above. We can officially recommend that you use that wonderful brain you parents possess (sleep deprived or otherwise) and make the best personal decision for your child and your needs.

~Care Instructions~

Hand wash in warm water with a mild detergent and let air dry. For small soiled spots a warm washcloth and mild detergent can be used to spot-treat your sheepskin. Brushing your sheepskin after it is dry will help return it to its original softness, though this isn't necessary. If you have any further questions on the care of your sheepskin please feel free to email me.

All sheepskins are approximately 36"L x 22"W
